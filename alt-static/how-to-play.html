🎮 How to Play

Everything you need to enjoy Porra Mundial 2026

1
Submit Your Predictions

Contact the game administrator before the deadline (June 10, 2026 at 23:59 UTC) and provide your picks for all the required categories:

  • 🏅 One winner per group (12 groups, A through L)
  • 🏟️ 4 teams that will reach the semi-finals
  • 🥈 2 teams that will play in the Final
  • 🏆 The World Cup champion
  • 🥉 Winner of the 3rd & 4th place match
  • 👟 Top Scorer (Golden Boot winner)
2
Watch the Tournament

The FIFA World Cup kicks off on June 11, 2026 in the USA, Mexico, and Canada. 48 teams will compete across 12 groups, then knock each other out through the Round of 32, Round of 16, Quarter-finals, Semi-finals, and finally the Grand Final.

3
Track Your Score

As the tournament progresses the scoreboard and results pages are updated. Points are awarded automatically once results become official.

✓ Correct pick = highlighted green ✗ Wrong pick = highlighted red ? Not decided yet = neutral
🏆
Win the Porra!

The player with the most points when the final whistle blows on July 19, 2026 wins. Good luck!
