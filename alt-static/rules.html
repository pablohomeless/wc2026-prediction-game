📋 Rules & Scoring

How the Porra Mundial 2026 works

🏅 Group Winners

Predict the winner of each of the 12 groups (A–L). You earn 1 point for each correctly predicted group winner.

Maximum: 1 pt × 12 groups = 12 points
🏟️ Semi-finalists

Pick 4 teams that you believe will reach the semi-finals. You earn 2 points for each team that actually makes it that far.

Maximum: 2 pt × 4 teams = 8 points
🥈 Finalists

Pick the 2 teams that will play in the Final. You earn 2 points for each correct finalist.

Maximum: 2 pt × 2 teams = 4 points
🌟 Special Picks
